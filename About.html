About the Creator

My name is Marissa Paris. I am 22 years old. I attend Our Lady of the Lake University and I created this website to give the students a little information about how to live a balanced life. The idea came from being around my younger sister and our cousin. They taught me all I know about meditating and balancing the chakras, and now I am sharing what I know with you. Hope it is helpful and inspires you to take a step in the meditating direction.

From personal experience, meditating helps me get through my day. I wake up and meditate for about five minutes to get my mind focused and ready for the day. When I go to work and take my lunch break, I go to my car and meditate for about 15 minutes to clear my mind.

My goal is not perfection, but simplicity. I want to live a happy, relaxed, simple life. I am not saying that stress and harships will not come my way, but meditating and balancing my chakras will help me to prepare for the those moments. Hopefully it will help you, too.
