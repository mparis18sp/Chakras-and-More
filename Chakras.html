All About Chakras

What is a Chakra?

Subtle energy flow in and out of us through the seven chakras. The spine is the stem and chakras are like flowers growing from this stem, through the body, and out a few inches in front of the body in the aura.

What is an Aura?

Everyone has an aura- whether you can see it or not. It is the energy that is dwelling outside our body. It is affected by the way we think, who we talk to, our environment, and so on. The condition of the aura affects the condition of the physical body and vice versa. Bring harmony and balance through chakras into the aura where it must have its reflection in the physical body.

7 Chakras

Root Chakra

Color: Red

Location: Base of spine near tailbone and goes up to bellow the belly button

Balanced: Sense of accomplishment and peace when you think about things like money, safety, and shelter.

Overactive: One may feel anxiety or jitteriness

How to Balance: Take care of survival needs for energy. Calm this chakra by meditating, praying, or connecting to spiritual guides.

Sacral Chakra

Color: Orange

Location: Right below the belly button and extends to its center.

Balanced: Relish in the pleasurable things life has to offer, without overdoing them.

Overactive: One will show addiction, gluttony, obesity, hormone imbalances, and restlessness.

How to Balance: Draw energy away from pleasure into your heart enjoying the fruits of your labor. Try creating a piece of art. Make love. Eat an enjoyable and healthy snack.

Solar Plexus Chakra

Color: Yellow

Location:

Balanced: One will feel a sense of wisdom, decisiveness, and personal power

Overactive: The power we have over our own lives extends into the lives of other people. Quick to anger, need to control and micromanage, greediness, and lack of compassion and empathy.

How to Balance: Open up your heart with love and compassion. Meditate on sending love and kindness from your heart to others.

Heart Chakra

Color: Green

Location: Right over your heart and radiates down to your breastbone and up to your throat.

Balanced: Able to equally feel love for yourself and others. Even when tough things happen, you can still see the compassion and kindness in others.

Overactive: One will lose their personal boundaries and start to make unhealthy choices. Puts the needs of others above themselves.

How to Balance: Take the love you give others and focus it on yourself. Take a relazing bath, get a massage, or meditate on sending compassion to yourself.

Throat Chakra

Color: Blue

Location: Between colar bone and radiates down to the center of yout heart and up to the center of your eyes.

Balanced: Clearly speaks with love, kindess, and truth. Knows what words are appropriate for each situation. Enlighten and inspire those around you.

Overactive: Spend a lot time trying to get your voice to be heard. Interrupts others, loud voice, "love to hear themselves talk.

How to Balance: Think before you speak. Is it true? Is it necessary? Is it kind?

Third Eye Chakra

Color: Indigo

Location: Center inbetween the eyebrows and radiates down to your mouth and up to the top of your head.

Balanced: One will feel in tune with the physical and material world. Receive psychic information from your 5 senses.

Overactive: Very unlikely to be overactive

How to Balance: Connect your body to the earth and repeat "I am a human being".

Crown Chakra

Color: Violet-white

Location: Top of head and radiates down between your eyes and then extends infinitely upward and otwardd, conneting you to the energy of the rest of the world.

Balanced: Equivalent to reaching nirvana like the Buddhist concept. Not only human anymore because you have conquered suffering and death.

Overactive: Not possible to live in the materia world and be overcome with consciousness energy.

How to Balance: Since no one has an overactive crown chakra, there is no need to balance it.
