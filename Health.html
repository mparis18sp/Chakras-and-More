How Does Health Relate to Meditation and Balancing Chakras?

After researching and reflecting on my own experiences, I have discovered that mediation can be related to health. There were so many ways, but here are the top 6:

Better management of stress, anxiety, and depression

Several studies sugest that mindfulness meditation can benefit those who struggle with stress, anxiety, and depression issues.

Increase Immunity

Onve study conducted at Ohio State University showed that regular mindfulness-based muscle relaxtion exerises lowered the risk of breast cancer recurring. Another study showed that the subjects demonstrated better resistance to viruses and tumors.

Lowered Blood Pressure

Mindfulness meditation has been shown to help control blood pressure. A study in the British Medical Journal showed that patients who meditated had considerably lower blood pressure than those in the control group.

Better Sleep

Meditation hellps the body learn to relax. It also trains the mind to settle the attention on an object such as te breath and allow other thoughts and emotions to float by like clouds on a beautiful day.

Relieving Symptoms of IBS

Irritable Bowl Syndrome is a common bowel disorder characterized by bloating, abdominal pain, cramps and altered bowel behavior. Daily relaxxing meditation has been found to relieve symptoms of IBS according to studies of The State University of New York.

Improving Happiness and General Wellbeing

According to a psychotherapist named Maria Camara,PhD, meditation helps us connect with our inner selves and true qualities. One someone accepts who they are and learn to work with their inner thoughts direcctly, something amazing is revealed: a loving capacity that has always been present within us.
